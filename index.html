Youth For Seva - Chiguru ’ 12

Day of Joy!

For the fourth consecutive year, Youth For Seva is organizing Chiguru, an inter-school competition for the government school children in the cultural arena.

Chiguru provides a platform for those not-so-priviledged tiny tots, who are mostly from the government schools, to give wings to their cultural prowess — be it dancing, singing, drawing, drama etc. It is a day dedicated to fulfilling the hidden dreams of these children through an inter-school competition.

This year, we are expecting more than 1200 children from 40 schools and orphanages to participate in the event.
